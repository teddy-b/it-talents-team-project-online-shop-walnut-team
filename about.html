About us

Welcome to Walnut Online Store, your number one source for technics. We’re dedicated to giving you the very best of products on the market, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness.

Founded in 2015 by IT Talents Walnut Team, Walnut Online Store has come a long way from its beginnings in Sofia, Bulgaria. When our team first started out, our passion for providing the best equipment for people drove us to do intense research, and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the fair trade wing of the tech industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
